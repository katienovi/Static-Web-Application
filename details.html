More about Krobus

Krobus is a shadow person who lives in the Sewers. He has a shop, aptly named Krobus' Shop, which is open all day, even during festivals.
You can access Krobus' shop after obtaining the Rusty Key from Gunther after donating 60 items to the Museum.

Becoming Roommates

Unlike the traditional way to marry someone in Stardew by building your relationship and gifting them a mermaid pendant, Krobus must be given a void ghost pendant instead. You can use this item to invite him to come be your roommate!
